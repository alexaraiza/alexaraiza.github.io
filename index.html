About me

Hello! I'm Alex, a software developer. I've always been interested in science and technology; as a kid, I loved getting my hands on anything that caught my interest and taking it apart to see how it worked. As an adult, I still love that. This curiosity led me to study engineering, where I discovered coding and instantly fell in love with how easy (and cheap!) it was to make and test my own ideas.

Over the years I've used my skills to develop workplace tools at BXYZ; improve the customer experience at Kombo, a startup in the transportation industry; develop a chat system for LineHayat, a student-run organization; and turn other projects into digital reality. Most recently I've been developing Anemo, an AI-powered web app to practice languages.

I focus mostly on back end development with Node.js, though I also have experience with Python, Rust, C++ and front end experience with React and Svelte.

Some of my interests include languages, music and photography.
